Radiation 3d Visualizations

This is a visualization for astrophysical radiation simulation data. There are two loosely coupled views of the data: A "point cloud preview" on the left and an isosurface detail view on the right (initially empty).

To generate the iso-surface matching the point cloud click the "Sync" button below the right panel. The isosurface may take a few seconds to appear.

To fit the data into the view click the "Position camera" button below the right panel. To rotate the views independently drag the mouse on either panel.

To change the iso-surface cutoff value adjust the slider on the top.

To limit the coordinate ranges adjust the limits sliders below the left panel.
